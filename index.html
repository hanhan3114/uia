About Sheep Plusie

A sheep plushie is a stuffed toy designed to resemble a sheep, often made from soft, plush fabric and filled with materials like polyester fiberfill.

Sheep plushies come in various forms, including realistic lambs, cartoonish representations like Shaun the Sheep, and even heatable lavender-scented plushies.
